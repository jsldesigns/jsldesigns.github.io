About Me

Industrial Designer

After bringing together a lot of my influences from my early years and surroundings, I feel as though I have developed a strong ambition to become a designer. I have a clear notion of the type of designer I want to be with my plans to both enter the product industry and create designs for web-based platforms. I would say, just up until recently I have learned many processes although I feel that as a designer there is always room to grow and improve. I believe that design is ever-growing and evolving as many new environments and trends arrive each year.
